The person behind the music

Chrissy Schroer

Writer · Veteran · Mother — Forever Still is her voice

I didn't stumble into this — I built it.

I'm a U.S. Army veteran who stepped away from the world for nearly a decade to rebuild my life and protect my children after surviving serious trauma. I chose safety. I chose survival. I built a quiet, stable life from nothing.

This year, I made the decision to step back into the world — publicly, creatively, and without hiding.

AI didn't make me an artist — it gave me a way to keep creating while raising two young boys on my own. The story, the emotion, and the meaning have always been mine.
