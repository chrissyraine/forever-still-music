About Forever Still

About Forever Still

Forever Still wasn't planned. It was built.

I'm a writer, a veteran, and a mother of two boys. Most of my life has been about figuring out how to keep going — through things that didn't make sense at the time, and didn't have words when they were happening.

That's where this came from.

Every song starts with something real. A moment, a conversation, a loss, a shift that stayed with me long enough that it had to become something else. I didn't set out to make music. I set out to make sense of what I had lived through.

AI is part of how I do that. It helps shape the sound, but it isn't the artist. It's the instrument. The story, the emotion, and the meaning have always been mine.

This project isn't about performance. It's about turning real experiences into something you can actually hear.

If something here feels familiar, you're probably not wrong.
