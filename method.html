Questions

Before you decide

Do I need to know anything about music or songwriting? ↓

Not at all. You just need to be willing to answer some honest questions about your story, your feelings, and what you want the song to hold. I handle everything else — the lyrics, the structure, the production.

How does the intake form work? ↓

After purchase, you'll receive a private link to your intake form by email. It walks you through a series of questions about who you are, what shaped you, what this song needs to hold, and the sound and feeling you're after. There's no wrong answer — the more real you are, the better the song.

How long does it take? ↓

Your song is delivered within 7 days of receiving your completed intake form. I take the time to do it right — this isn't a template, it's built from scratch for you.

What does "AI production" mean? Is this still personal? ↓

Yes. AI is the instrument — I'm the one playing it. The lyrics come from your story, shaped by me. The sound direction, the emotional choices, the production decisions — those are mine. AI is how I produce music independently. The heart of the song is entirely human: yours and mine.

What do I receive when it's done? ↓

You receive three things: your custom song as an MP3 file, your full lyric sheet, and a written piece that explains the story behind the song — where each section came from and what it means. Everything arrives by email.

Can I use the song for a funeral, wedding, or anniversary? ↓

Yes — and those are some of the most meaningful ways people use these songs. Played at a memorial, given as a gift at an anniversary, sung at a celebration. The song is yours to use however the moment calls for it.
