Questions

Before you decide

What's the difference between the three tiers? ↓

The Method is a workbook you use yourself with AI. Your Life, Your Song is me writing and producing it for you. The Full Experience is the same plus a lyric video and printed keepsake. Same heart. Different levels of involvement and finish.

How long does the done-for-you service take? ↓

14 days from your completed intake for Your Life, Your Song. 10 days for the Full Experience. The timeline starts the moment you submit your form — not when you purchase.

What if I'm not a writer? What if my story is messy? ↓

Good. Messy and real beats polished and performative every time. The intake walks you through it — you don't need to know what to say. You just need to say it.

Will my song sound like your music? ↓

It will sound like Forever Still — that's the workflow. Cinematic, emotional, built for the weight of real moments. If you want a completely different genre or production style, this isn't the right service.

Can I share my song or use it commercially? ↓

You own your copy for personal use — play it, share it with family, post it on social media, use it at events. Commercial use (selling it, monetized streaming, sync licensing) requires a separate agreement. Reach out and we'll talk.

What if I'm not happy with my song? ↓

I'll do one revision pass on Your Life, Your Song and the Full Experience. I'll take your specific feedback and adjust — a section that doesn't feel right, a tone that's off, a detail that's missing. Beyond that, the Method exists for a reason: some songs only the writer can finish.

Is this really written by AI? ↓

AI is the production tool, not the author. Your story is the source material. The lyrics and emotional architecture are mine — or yours, if you're using the Method. I've been transparent about this since day one. It's the whole point of Forever Still.
